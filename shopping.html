Places to Shop in SWFL

Gulf coast town center

With an initial launch in 2005, Gulf Coast Town Center "Kicked off" the expansion in estero by brining a lot of brand name stores and restraunts right by Florida Gulf Coast University and neighborhoods like Miromar Lakes, Grandezza, etc. There are many diffrent notable shopping brands like Staples, Petco, Babies "R" Us and for a night out at the cinema there is a Regal Cinema visible directly from I-75.

More About Gulf Coast Town Center

Miromar Outlets

Voted the "Best Shopping Mall and Shopping District" in Southwest Florida, Miromar Outlets is one of the largest and best shopping locations in the area. Miromar features over 140 diffrent brand name outlets including Saks Fifth Avenue OFF 5TH, Neiman Marcus Last Call, Bloomingdale’s The Outlet Store, Nike Factory Store, Polo Ralph Lauren Factory Store, Tommy Hilfiger & More! Find out more about Miromar Outlets and why it's been voted "Best Shopping Experience" 20 years in a row!

More About Miromar Outlets

Coconut Point

The Coconut Point mall first opened its door in November of 2006 with over 120 stores directly in the heart of Estero FL. Coconut is located directly off of US 41 and includes many brand name favorites such as: Dillard's, SuperTarget, Best Buy, Office Depot, PetSmart, Total Wine & More, TJ Maxx, Michaels, Home Centric, Ross Dress for Less, DSW, Ulta Beauty, West Elm, Regal Cinemas & More! There are many communities surronding the mall which makes it a key destination for all who visit Bonita Springs or Estero.

More About Coconut Point Mall

Promenade at Bonita Bay

The Promenade is an upscalled outdoor shopping center right next to Bonita Springs's oldest community, Bonita Bay. This center features fine dining and luxurious shopping. Some key locations to note are DeRomo's Italian Dining, Roy's, The Center Bar, Little Paris, Coastal Outfitters & More! For those who are looking for a higher quality of experience, the Promenade at Bonita Bay is surely a location that will impress.

More About The Promenade

Edison Mall

Edison Mall is one of the oldest shopping locations in Southwest Florida that still sees millions of visitors each year. Originally opening its doors in 1965, the Edison Mall was and still is the premiere shopping desination for those who live farther north in Fort Myers FL. The mall holds 133+ stores and has recieved multiple updates to stay in tune with what shoppers are looking for today. If you want a nice break from the heat while shopping at your favorite stores, Edison Mall is a great destination for you!
